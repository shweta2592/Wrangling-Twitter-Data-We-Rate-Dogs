Insights¶

  • In the chart on Ratings over time,we observe that, on an average, the ratings over time is observed to be around 1.05 with dips observed in few months. Maximum drop in rating was observed in March, 2017.

  • In the chart on Retweets and Favorites over time, we see that in July,2016, the number of favorites were at the peak with a whopping count of 160000. The number of retweets have always been less compared to favorites. The highest count in retweets was recorded in the month of June 2017. On an average ,the number of favorites for all the tweets over time were approximately 8100 and the avg retweets were around 2700. So, we can safely say that for every one retweet, there will be 3 likes.

  • In the chart on Dog Rating Scores We see that majority of the scores lie between the ratio 1.1 and 1.2 with the mean being 1.05 and median being 1.1 meaning it is left skewed.Also, we observe that the kurtosis is high with most of the datapoints lying between 1.1 and 1.2. Majority of the dogs have been rated good with few exceptions where the dogs have been rated 0.

  • From the charts on Dog Breeds , it is evident that Golden Labrador is the most popular dog with maximum number of likes, retweets and high ratings followed by Labrador retriever.

  • From the chart on Visualizing Dog Stages, it looks like dogger,pupper and puppo get the maximum ratings. On an average doggo gets rated better than the rest of the stages.

  • From the chart on Gender, what we see is from the available genders, Females get rated better than the Males.
